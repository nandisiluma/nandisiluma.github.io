LingoMate is a web app that fosters language learning through user-user interaction. Language is not just memorized words, it's a culture and and an experience. Users can learn more from native speakers of that language. Users are matched based on the languages they are interested in trading. For example, one user who speaks Ndebele and is interested in learning Spanish can be matched with a user interested in Ndebele and proficient in Spanish. The goal is to provide a fun and holistic way of adopting new languages.
